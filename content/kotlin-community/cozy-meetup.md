Cozy Kotlin MeetUp is a informal gathering for Kotlin enthusiasts organized by [Community.kt](/kotlin-community/).

## Schedule

We do monthly meetups in Prague, Czech Republic, and occasional meetups in other cities across Europe.

## What to expect

A relaxed atmosphere where you can share your use cases and real-life scenarios, or learn what others are working on. No presentations, no formal agenda, just great conversations.

## Who's invited

All Kotlin enthusiasts. Any experience level, any use case, any background.
